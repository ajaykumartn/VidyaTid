## Subscription Tiers

**Daily Query Limit:** 10 queries per day

- ✓ 2 quiz questions per session
- ✗ No diagram access
- ✗ No previous year papers
- ✗ No image-based doubt solving
- ✗ No mock tests
- ✗ No progress tracking

**Best For:** Students who want to try the platform before committing to a paid plan.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the VidyaTid website
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. You'll automatically start with a Free tier subscription

### Upgrading to a Paid Plan

1. **From the Pricing Page:**
   - Click "Pricing" in the navigation menu
   - Compare the features of each tier
   - Click "Subscribe" on your desired plan
   - Complete the payment process

2. **From Your Dashboard:**
   - Go to your account dashboard
   - Click "Upgrade Plan" button
   - Select your desired tier
   - Complete the payment process

3. **When You Hit a Feature Gate:**
   - Try to access a premium feature
   - A modal will appear showing which plans include that feature
   - Click "Upgrade Now"
   - Select your desired tier and complete payment

### Payment Process

1. Select your desired subscription tier
2. Review the plan details and pricing
3. Click "Proceed to Payment"
4. You'll be redirected to Razorpay's secure payment gateway
5. Choose your payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Net Banking
   - UPI
   - Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.)
6. Complete the payment
7. You'll receive instant access to your new tier features
8. A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address

## Upgrading Your Subscription

### How Upgrades Work

**Immediate Activation:** When you upgrade to a higher tier, your new features are activated immediately.

**Prorated Billing:** You only pay the difference between your current plan and the new plan, adjusted for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

**Example:**
- Current Plan: Starter (₹99/month)
- Days Remaining: 15 days
- Upgrade To: Premium (₹299/month)
- Prorated Credit: ₹49.50 (15 days of Starter)
- Amount to Pay: ₹149.50 - ₹49.50 = ₹100

### Steps to Upgrade

1. Go to your dashboard or pricing page
2. Click "Upgrade" on your desired tier
3. Review the prorated pricing
4. Click "Confirm Upgrade"
5. Complete the payment
6. Enjoy immediate access to new features!

---

## Downgrading Your Subscription

### How Downgrades Work

**Scheduled Downgrade:** When you downgrade to a lower tier, the change is scheduled for your next billing cycle.

**Current Access Maintained:** You keep access to your current tier features until your subscription end date.

**No Refunds:** Downgrades don't provide refunds for the current billing period.

### Steps to Downgrade

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Select "Downgrade Plan"
4. Choose your desired lower tier
5. Confirm the downgrade
6. You'll see "Scheduled downgrade to [Tier] on [Date]"
7. Continue using your current tier until the end date
8. On the end date, your subscription automatically switches to the lower tier

### Canceling a Scheduled Downgrade

If you change your mind before the downgrade takes effect:

1. Go to your dashboard
2. You'll see "Scheduled downgrade to [Tier]"
3. Click "Cancel Downgrade"
4. Your subscription will continue at the current tier

## Canceling Your Subscription

### How Cancellation Works

**Access Until End Date:** When you cancel, you retain access to your paid features until your subscription end date.

**Auto-Renewal Disabled:** Your subscription won't automatically renew.

**Downgrade to Free:** After your subscription ends, you'll be downgraded to the Free tier.

### Steps to Cancel

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm your cancellation
5. You'll receive a confirmation email
6. Continue using paid features until your end date
7. After the end date, you'll automatically move to the Free tier

### Reactivating After Cancellation

If you cancel but want to reactivate before your subscription ends:

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Reactivate Subscription"
3. Your auto-renewal will be re-enabled
4. No additional payment needed until the next billing cycle

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Issues

#### Payment Failed

**Common Causes:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card limit exceeded
- Bank declined the transaction
- Incorrect card details
- Network timeout

**Solutions:**
1. Verify your card details are correct
2. Check your account balance
3. Contact your bank to authorize the transaction
4. Try a different payment method
5. Wait a few minutes and try again
6. Contact support if the issue persists

#### Payment Deducted But Subscription Not Activated

**What to do:**
1. Wait 5-10 minutes for the webhook to process
2. Refresh your dashboard
3. Check your email for confirmation
4. If still not activated after 30 minutes, contact support with:
   - Transaction ID
   - Payment screenshot
   - Registered email address

#### Duplicate Charge

**What to do:**
1. Check if you clicked "Pay" multiple times
2. Verify in your bank statement (pending vs. completed)
3. Contact support immediately with transaction IDs
4. We'll investigate and process a refund if confirmed

---

### Usage Issues

#### Queries Not Resetting

**Expected Behavior:**
- Queries reset daily at midnight UTC (5:30 AM IST)

**If not resetting:**
1. Check the current time and timezone
2. Refresh your browser
3. Log out and log back in
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### Reached Daily Limit

**What to do:**
- Wait until midnight UTC for reset (5:30 AM IST)
- Upgrade to a higher tier for more queries
- Ultimate tier has unlimited queries

#### Feature Not Accessible

**Check:**
1. Your current subscription tier
2. Whether the feature is included in your tier
3. If your subscription is active (not expired)
4. If you've reached any usage limits

**Solutions:**
- Upgrade to a tier that includes the feature
- Renew your subscription if expired
- Wait for usage limits to reset

---

### Account Issues

#### Can't Log In

**Solutions:**
1. Verify your email and password
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check if your account is verified
4. Clear browser cookies and cache
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact support if locked out

#### Subscription Shows Expired But I Paid

**What to do:**
1. Check your payment confirmation email
2. Verify the payment was successful in your bank
3. Check if the payment date matches your renewal date
4. Contact support with payment proof

#### Not Receiving Emails

**Check:**
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Email address is correct in your profile
3. Email provider isn't blocking our emails

**Solutions:**
1. Add support@vidyatid.com to your contacts
2. Whitelist our domain
3. Update your email address in settings
4. Contact support to resend emails

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I try premium features before subscribing?**
A: The Free tier lets you experience the core platform. For premium features, we recommend starting with the Starter plan (₹99/month) which includes diagrams and previous papers.

**Q: Can I switch between plans anytime?**
A: Yes! You can upgrade anytime with immediate effect. Downgrades are scheduled for your next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I don't renew my subscription?**
A: Your subscription will expire, and you'll be downgraded to the Free tier. You'll still have access to basic features with 10 queries per day.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: We don't currently offer a pause feature. You can cancel and resubscribe later, but you'll lose access to paid features during the gap.

A: Currently, our pricing is already student-friendly. We may introduce verified student discounts in the future.
